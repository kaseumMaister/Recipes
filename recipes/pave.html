Pave

Ingredients

  • 4 freshly squeezed limes and zest of 1
  • 400g sweetened condensed milk (1can)
  • 300ml double cream
  • 700g digestive biscuits
  • 1 cup of milk
  • A sprinkle of chocolate powder

Directions

  • 1.Squeeze the juice from the limes into a bowl and zest a lime.
  • 2.Pour the condensed milk, double cream and lime juice into a blender and blend for 1-2 minutes.
  • 3.Pour the milk into a bowl and dip each biscuit in the milk. Grab your bowl and lie with a thin layer of biscuits, then add a layer of the mixture on top (about 2 large spoonfuls).
  • 4.Repeat this process until the mixture is used.
  • 5.Sprinkle the top layer with chocolate powder and lime zest.
  • 6.Leave in the fridge for at least 3 hours, ideally leave for 6 hours and voila. Bom apetite.
